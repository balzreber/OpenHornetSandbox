◆ DCSBIOS_RS485_SLAVE

#define DCSBIOS_RS485_SLAVE   2

DCS BIOS Slave ID

The following #define tells DCS-BIOS that this is a RS-485 slave device. It also sets the address of this slave device. Since this is for the 1A2A Master Arm Panel the slave address is 2, according to 1A2A.

◆ TXENABLE_PIN

#define TXENABLE_PIN   2

TX Enable Pin

The Arduino pin that is connected to the /RE and DE pins on the RS-485 transceiver. This is Pin 2 in all ABSIS NANO boards.
